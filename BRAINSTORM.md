Kontras skena dulu vs sekarang.

- Sekarang: algoritma, emo night, gelombang baru kayak Home Is Where

### 3. "The Emo Starter Pack: Musik, Fashion & Attitude"
Guide praktis buat newbie.
- Band wajib dengerin
- Outfit formula
- Sejarah singkat genre
- **Nilai jual**: konten praktis -> orang rela bayar

### 4. "Confessions of a Former Scene Kid"
Personal narrative.
- Cerita pertama masuk skena emo
- Konser pertama, first band t-shirt
- Drama temen-temen skena
- **Nilai jual**: relatability

### 5. "The Anatomy of Emo Lyrics"
Breakdown lirik emo classic.
- Kenapa orang emo suka nangis
- Puisi & storytelling dalam lagu
- Unsur edukasi

## Sesi 2 — Top 5 Band Emo Paling Influential 2000s

1. **My Chemical Romance** — game changer, *Black Parade* abadi
2. **Taking Back Sunday** — drama kings, *Tell All Your Friends* iconic
3. **Dashboard Confessional** — bawa emo ke MTV akustik, rajanya galau
4. **Fall Out Boy** — jembatan emo ke mainstream, *From Under the Cork Tree* monster
5. **Jimmy Eat World** — pionir yang ngebuka pintu, *Bleed American* starting point

Honorable mentions: Brand New, The Used, Thursday, Hawthorne Heights, **Saosin**

### Saosin?
Masuk honorable mention, bukan top 5. Saosin technically lebih post-hardcore/screamo — influence ke teknikalitas musik (vocal runs, riff complex) bukan emotional storytelling & fashion identitas. Top 10 bisa duduk di 7-8.

## Emo vs Post-Hardcore (Parsing buat Narasi Ebook)

| Emo | Post-Hardcore |
|-----|---------------|
| Lirik personal/emosional, melody-driven, storytelling | Turunan hardcore punk, lebih agresif |
| Fashion sebagai identitas | Breakdown, screaming |
| MCR, Dashboard, TBS awal, Jimmy Eat World | Saosin, Underoath, Thursday, Alexisonfire, Silverstein |

Parsing tipis karena satu pohon keluarga. Tapi biar fokus narasi gak melebar.
